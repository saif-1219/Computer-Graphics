Transformation Controls

(a) Rotation around axes

Q - X-axis counter-clockwise | W - X-axis clockwise

A - Y-axis counter-clockwise | S - Y-axis clockwise

Z - Z-axis counter-clockwise | X - Z-axis clockwise

(b) Translation (shift)

← / → - Move left/right (X-axis)

↑ / ↓ - Move up/down (Y-axis)

O - Move forward (Z+) | P - Move backward (Z-)

(c) Scaling (shrink/grow)

E - Grow X | R - Shrink X

D - Grow Y | F - Shrink Y

C - Grow Z | V - Shrink Z

(d) Reflection about axes

1 - Toggle X reflection | 2 - Toggle Y reflection | 3 - Toggle Z reflection

(e) Shear transformation

, / . - XY Shear (negative/positive)

/ / [ - XZ Shear (negative/positive)

L / ; - YZ Shear (negative/positive)

(f) Reset

M - Reset all transformations
